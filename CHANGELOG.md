## [2.5.0] - 2026-05-28

**Early Access Test Release** — First release shared with external testers via Google Drive and GitHub.

This is a proprietary build (not open source).

### Highlights

**Major UFPM Improvements**
- Complete redesign of the Member Profile screen (reached via "View Profile" from the roster).
  - Much more compact layout — less scrolling required.
  - Prominent color-coded score chip in the header.
  - Workflow-focused panels for "Test" and "AI Plan" that surface relevant member context.
  - New inline history mini-chart + summary statistics row (Entries / Latest / Best / Trend).
  - Significantly improved information density while staying readable.
- Broader UFPM navigation and roster management refactor for better performance and usability with larger rosters.

**History & Visualization**
- Added bar chart visualization option in the History view.
- General improvements to history list density and interaction.

**AI Training Plans**
- Added pregnancy / postpartum mode with specialized prompting and safety guardrails.
- Improved adherence to the Warfighter's Fitness Playbook structure and frequency guidelines.

**Other**
- Updated app branding to "PT Ready".
- Various bug fixes around scoring, pregnancy mode, and UI polish.

### Notes for Testers

- This is a **test build**. Expect some rough edges.
- iOS version is still in active development.
- All data is stored locally on your device.
- Feedback is extremely valuable at this stage — especially around the new Member Profile experience and AI plan quality.
